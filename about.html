About Our Company

Premium Oil from Tanzanian Fields to Your Kitchen

Kwanza Oil is a premium sunflower oil brand dedicated to delivering high-quality, pure, and healthy cooking oil to households and businesses. We focus on providing nutritious, affordable, and locally produced oil that meets modern health standards while supporting local agriculture.

Our Production Process

Step - 01

Seed Selection & Farm Preparation

We carefully select high-quality sunflower seeds and prepare fertile land to ensure strong, healthy crop growth.

Step - 02

Sunflower Cultivation

Our crops are grown using best farming practices, ensuring proper care, nutrition and natural growth for premium quality.

Step - 03

Harvesting & Seed Collection

Sunflowers are harvested at the right time and seeds are carefully collected to preserve their freshness and oil quality.

Step - 04

Oil Extraction & Processing

The seeds are processed in aclean, modern facility to produce pure, high quality sunflower oil that meets safety standards.
